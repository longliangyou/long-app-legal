Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Welcome to Numem ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by Numem.

1. Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally: Your game progression, high scores, and settings are stored locally on your device using standard Apple frameworks (such as UserDefaults). We do not transmit or collect this gameplay data on our servers.

In-App Purchases: Numem offers subscriptions and in-app purchases. All transactions are securely processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID credentials. We only receive anonymous receipts from Apple to verify your purchase status and unlock premium features.

Analytics and Crash Reports: If you have opted in to share diagnostics with App Developers via your iOS Settings, Apple provides us with anonymized crash reports and basic usage metrics. This helps us improve the app's stability. No personally identifiable information is included in these reports.

2. How We Use Information

We use the minimal aggregated, anonymous data we receive solely to:

  • Verify subscriptions and unlock features.
  • Identify and fix bugs or performance issues.
  • Improve the overall user experience of Numem.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs that track your behavior across other apps and websites.

4. Children's Privacy

Numem does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information.

5. Game Center

If you choose to use Apple's Game Center for achievements and leaderboards, your game data (such as scores) is processed and stored by Apple in accordance with their privacy policy. We do not collect or store this Game Center data on our own servers.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at forallandiyou@gmail.com.
